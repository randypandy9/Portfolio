Further Information

How many bikes do you have in total?
Up to 100 bikes.

Can I make a multiple booking?
Yes. You can book multiple times.

Can I buy other equipment?
No. We only hire out bikes.

Do I need insurance?
We don't offer insurance but it's your responsibility to make sure your insurance policy covers accidental damage to bikes if you wish.

Do I have to clean my bike before I return it?
No. We will do this for you as each bike is serviced after each use.

How do I pay for my bike?
You will enter your card details on our website but the payment will not be taken out until you have come and collected your bike from us.

What happens if something goes wrong with my bike?
You can either return it to us for repair; this will take 1 working day, or service it yourself using a bike repair kit.

Is there a penalty if I return my bike late?
Yes, we charge a late-return fee for each bike which is returned late. A £5 charge for the first day and £3 for subsequent days.

Further Information
If you are not from the UK or are unaware of cycling laws, have a look at the information below:

  • It is illegal to cycle on the pavement. However, police may show discretion to younger children cycling on the pavement for whom cycling on the road would not be a safe option.
  • It is illegal to cycle in the dark withouth lights (these should be provided with your hire bike).
  • Helmets are not compulsory.
  • You should normally cycle on the left-hand side when riding on the public highway.
  • It is legal to cycle two abreast on a road but it is considered courteous to cycle single file when cars are trying to overtake you.
